Ego Entertainment LLC is a premier entertainment company based out of Atlanta, Georgia that specializes in event planning, promotions and strategic marketing. Founded in May of 2008 by Fred Clark, Eric Briggs, Bryant “Chuck” Bourgeois and Christina Robinson, Ego has risen to prominence within the collegiate community. We are a multifaceted company that strives to continuously raise the bar of the nightlife experience, while remaining socially conscious. While boasting a staff of over 50 college alumni and students, we have been able to capture the support of many young adults in the Atlanta area. Through extensive market research and strong personal relationships, Ego has been able to specifically tailor our events to the needs of our clientele.

Ego Entertainment prides ourselves on having a unique staff that can imagine and create the newest and innovative event concepts and ideas. Our unique marketing tactics, which are constantly evolving with time, have captured the attention of many young adults who support our vision. We aspire to make each night spent with Ego Entertainment an experience that will stay with the customer for years to come. We have worked with many fraternities, sororities, radio personalities, entertainers and other prominent groups in order to ensure our customers enjoy the luxury of the best experience possible.
